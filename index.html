Saideh Ahangary

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the computer science department at the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam, My research focuses on designing “correct-by-construction” networks that offer provable guarantees on correctness, reliability, and security (e.g., proving that network invariants such as path reachability hold, or that routing “loops” are absent, or that link utilizations never exceed predefined thresholds). To this end, my work investigates the use of formal verification methods and techniques to tackle these challenges.
